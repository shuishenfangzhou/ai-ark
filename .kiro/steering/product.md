# Product Overview

## AI Tools Dashboard

A comprehensive AI tools navigation platform that helps users discover and explore the latest AI productivity tools. The platform serves as a centralized directory for AI tools across various categories including writing, image generation, video creation, coding, and office productivity.

### Key Features

- **Tool Discovery**: Browse 283+ curated AI tools across 15 categories
- **Smart Search**: Real-time search with filtering by category, pricing, and popularity
- **Detailed Profiles**: Each tool includes description, pricing, ratings, and official links
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for both desktop and mobile experiences
- **Social Features**: User authentication, favorites, and tool submission

### Target Audience

- AI enthusiasts and early adopters
- Content creators and digital marketers
- Developers and technical professionals
- Business professionals seeking productivity tools
- Students and researchers

### Business Model

- Free tool discovery and browsing
- Premium features through WeChat integration
- Tool submission and promotion services
- Affiliate partnerships with featured tools

The platform aims to be the go-to destination for discovering and evaluating AI tools in the Chinese market.
